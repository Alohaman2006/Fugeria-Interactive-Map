Fortress – Skógarborg

Shape / Location: Roughly square, tucked in dense forest at the foot of a mountain

Condition: Active, lightly manned

Age: 221 Years

Purpose: Acts as a forest and mountain guardpost; provides shelter from beasts

Layout: Thick square stone walls; small keep; inner timber barracks; single gate facing the main path

Surroundings: Pine forests, mountain slope behind; small hunting paths lead to the fort

Notes: Mostly a seasonal refuge; snow and tree cover make it difficult to spot from a distance

Fortress – Sléttborg

Shape / Location: Large, round, in plains with gentle hills

Condition: Active; busiest fort in Sverday

Age: 148 Years

Purpose: Regional hub for travelers, caravans, and temporary camps; central coordination post

Layout: Circular wall; central tower; many tents and temporary shelters inside; inner courtyard with multiple fire pits; minimal permanent housing

Surroundings: Open plains with scattered hills; crossroads for multiple routes

Notes: Unlike other forts, more of a “travel hub” than a pure defensive structure; always busy with merchants, travelers, and mercenaries
